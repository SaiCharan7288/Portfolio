My Projects

SPECTRUM

Spectrum is an inclusive platform connecting differently-abled individuals and the LGBTQIA+ community with job opportunities.It bridges the gap between employers and underrepresented talent, fostering diversity and equality in workplaces. The goal is to ensure everyone has access to meaningful and dignified employment.

MINDFUL HAVEN

Mindful Haven is a supportive platform designed to help people struggling with mental health challenges.. It offers resources, guidance, and a safe space to promote healing and well-being. The goal is to empower individuals on their journey to better mental health.

MEDSONO

Medsono is a telemedicine platform that connects patients with healthcare professionals for convenient, online consultations.. It simplifies access to medical care, offering a seamless way to address health concerns from the comfort of home. The goal is to make healthcare accessible and efficient for everyone.
